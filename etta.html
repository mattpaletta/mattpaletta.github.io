Etta - The Paletta Keyboard

Finally, a keyboard made for the 21st century!

Stop living in the past! ETTA offers a new keyboard called the Paletta keyboard which has a unique layout designed with the iPhone user in mind. For those of us who type on our iPhone with one hand, the Paletta keyboard will make typing easier! This new keyboard layout has the most commonly used keys on the side of the keyboard you are using. The Paletta keyboard allows you to access your favourite emojis easily and is extremely customizable.

Privacy

  • We know the things you type on your phone are personal, ETTA keeps your private information private.
  • To help with spelling and to predict words when typing, ETTA stores words you type on the device. This data is not uploaded to any server or stored anywhere other than on your device.
